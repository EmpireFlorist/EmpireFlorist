About Us

Florist has a variety of collections to meet your taste that will enhance each occasion you choose while creating an ever refreshing sentiment that will bring unforgettable memories. Our tailor-made designs and ever increasing desire to cater your needs are being constantly explored.
Since 1986, Empire Florist has positioned as one of the leading trend in floristry. Founded by Ooi and Lee, the company is conveniently located at Pekan Simpang Kuala. Within the few years we have progressed and diversified our services. Apart from floral designing, we also offer our expertise in event venues decoration.
In year 2000, Empire Florist has been certified and approved as the Interflora Nominated Florist by Interflora Pacific Unit Limited. With the leading of professional team, Empire Florist provides an impressive range of appealing options in floral design arrangements and site decorations to enhance any occasion or event.
Empire florist trained sales and design team has its own imagination to enhance your gift, party or event distinctly yours. Let our professionals help you to select creative floral designs; flowering or green plants; custom dried or silk arrangements; fruit, gift and gourmet baskets or a unique gift.
Our wedding and party consultation team will help you organise a themed party for any age, a memorable wedding scheme or a dramatic banquet for your business or organization. On top of that, we are willing to assist you in your home or business landscaping.
As a flower connoisseur, the Empire Florist team believes in delivering beauty blended with stylish, elegance and poetic appearances where you invariably for long.
